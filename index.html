about

Hello, my name is Doug and I'm a Web Developer...

I began teaching myself web development as a curious hobby, which perpetuated into a passion I have decided to direct my career path down. I have always been both creative and logical, and nothing puts both of these skills to use together as well as designing well-functioning and aesthetic websites/apps. As a result, I have taken to the field like a seal to a beachball.

Like most of us. I started by trying to learn almost every programming language I had heard of, until I was drawn like gravity into specialising JavaScript, and then even deeper into React. Now I consider myself to be fully MERN stacked and have can build CRUD applications from the ground up as a second nature.

While I do boast knowing how to work the front and back end of CRUD application, it is the front-end where my skills are put to their best use. I enjoy playing with new animation packages and unique skills using vanilla JavaScript and out-of-the-box CSS.

I study Python like a hobby. I am in no way an expert, just want to know about it because it is clearly a very influential and growing programming language. I consider it to be the piano or Latin of languages because it gives and excellent foundation of knowledge which can be applied to other languages.

I am already skilled enough in React to be helping out newcomers, which has ignited my further ambition of teaching programming one day. This will be both online and in person. Which is another reason why I make sure I keep a sufficient knowledge of Python.
